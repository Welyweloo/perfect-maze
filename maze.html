Rules

Please read carefully, once you start the rules will disappear.

The game starts at the orange cell, you need to escape reaching the purple cell. You will play with your keyboard:

- Press the arrow up to go up

- Press the arrow down to go down

- Press the arrow right to go forward

- Press the arrow left to go backward

Also, you can't escape the maze !
